Is this a short-term rental in Salzburg?

ELS_Apartment is a furnished monthly rental in Elsbethen, directly south of Salzburg. Stays range from 29 nights to six months.

Who is the apartment for?

It is designed for business travellers, relocating employees, project staff and corporate guests. It is not marketed as tourist accommodation.

What is included in the monthly rent?

The furnished apartment includes utilities, heating, 5G internet, a full kitchen, private washing machine and a mountain bike.

How well is it connected to Salzburg?

Bus routes 160, 165 and 7 stop nearby. Red Bull Base is a 5–7 minute walk, Salzburg Old Town about 15 minutes by bus.
